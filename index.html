    About Us

    At SonLife Community Church, we believe in the power of faith and community. Our regular worship services are a time for prayer, uplifting music, and inspiring teachings that help deepen your relationship with God. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can experience the love and grace of Christ. Whether you're new to faith or looking to grow in your spiritual journey, our doors are open to you.

    • Spirit-filled worship

      Experience Spirit-filled worship, life-transforming teachings, and the prophetic voice in the house, drawing you closer to God and our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ, each week.

    • Transform Lives

      Join us as we seek the power and presence of the Holy Spirit to ignite faith, bring healing, and transform lives.

    • Grow in faith

      Together, let’s grow in faith, pursue holiness, and witness the mighty works of God through prayer, worship, and the Word.

    In addition to our worship services, we are committed to serving our local community through various outreach programs and events. We offer educational opportunities, including Bible studies and classes for all ages, to help you explore and understand scripture more deeply. Join us at SonLife Community Church, where we not only gather to worship but also work together to make a positive impact in our community and beyond!

    Welcome to SonLife
    Pentecostal Church!

    We look forward to welcoming you to SonLife Pentecostal Church, where you can be yourself, encounter God, and experience His Spirit, healing, and blessings in a powerful way!

    1. What will the Sunday morning service be like?

    Our Sunday services begin at 10:00 am and conclude around noon. Each service is contemporary and Spirit-led, centered on the Word of God, with a strong emphasis on the Holy Spirit’s power. We come together to seek God’s presence, open our hearts for healing blessings, and experience prophetic encounters that uplift and renew.

    2. What is the worship music style?

    Our worship is Spirit-filled, combining contemporary and traditional music in a way that invites the congregation into a deep, authentic encounter with God. Led by our worship team, the music creates a space where you can freely worship and feel the presence of the Holy Spirit moving, bringing peace, strength, and joy.

    3. Is there a program for my children?

    Yes! Kids Life and Youth Life programs run year-round, creating a safe, joyful space for children and youth of all ages—from Nursery to Junior High. We help them experience the power of the Holy Spirit, learn about God’s love, and grow in faith through Spirit-filled teachings and engaging activities.

    4. What should I wear?

    SonLife welcomes you as you are, whether in a suit, jeans, or whatever makes you comfortable. We believe God looks at the heart, so come in what feels right for you.
